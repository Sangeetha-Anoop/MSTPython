Welcome To Our Kindergarten Where The Adventure Begins!

Our kindergarten provides an international education in a bilingual environment. Here at KIDS CLUB School we support the development of the whole child through inquiry, exploration and play-centered learning. Our goal is to meet the academic, social, emotional, physical and cultural needs of every child. Through the IB Primary Years Programme the children are encouraged to view their world through an internationally-minded lens.
